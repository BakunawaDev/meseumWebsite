Crossing the boundaries between art and design, Taguig-based artist Avel Efondo crafts vibrant and unique light objects that bring timeless energy. His creations aim to invoke a special sensation to the observer resulting in a lasting impression. This viewing room presents Avel pieces from the artist's celebrated Ocean Drive Collection - a limited series of neon sculptures, alongside most recent work from the studio. These works highlight Key's interest in building new forms and environments through exploring classical design and reimagining traditional techniques. Avel's background and training in architecture (B.A., University of the Philippines, College of Arts) provides firm foundation and inspiration for his interdisciplinary practice.
